How It Works

Application Process

  1. 1

    Submit Application

    Send your resume and cover letter through our contact form, specifying your preferred track.

  2. 2

    Review & Shortlisting

    Our team reviews applications and shortlists candidates based on fit and availability.

  3. 3

    Interview / Assessment

    Shortlisted candidates participate in an interview or complete a brief assessment task.

  4. 4

    Onboarding & Project Work

    Welcome orientation, mentor assignment, and active participation in assigned projects with regular check-ins.

  5. 5

    Completion & Certification

    Final presentation, feedback session, and awarding of a verified completion certificate.

Credentials

Verified Certificates

Upon successful completion, interns receive an official Amaanitvam Foundation certificate with a unique ID. Employers and institutions can instantly verify authenticity through our portal.

Certificate IDs follow the format AF-YYYY-XXXX and are registered in our secure verification database.
